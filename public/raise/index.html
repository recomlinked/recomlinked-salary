Common questions

Is this just ChatGPT?+
No. ChatGPT can write a generic script if you spend 20 minutes prompting it correctly. This is a structured coaching flow that maps your specific situation to 15+ factors, then gives you a personalised plan and remembers every conversation for 30 days as you prepare.
How accurate is the probability score?+
The score is an estimate based on your specific situation and market conditions, not a guarantee. It's calibrated on the structural factors that actually drive raise outcomes. It's designed to be directionally accurate so you know whether you're in a strong position, a weak one, or somewhere in between, and exactly what moves it.
What if I'm not sure about some of the answers?+
Pick the closest option. The coaching chat handles the nuance: if you're unsure about your market rate, your manager's position, or when exactly your review is, the coach will help you figure it out.
Will my employer find out I'm using this?+
No. Nothing you type here leaves this site. We don't share data, and we don't sell anything. You can delete everything with one click from your dashboard after you've bought the plan.
What does the 30-day coaching look like?+
After you purchase, you get a personal link (valid 30 days). Come back whenever you need. Your coach remembers every conversation in that window. Week 1 is usually preparation and evidence-gathering. Week 2 is practice conversations and role-play. Week 3 is the actual negotiation. Week 4 is follow-up and counter-offer handling. You're never reading dry advice. You're working through a specific plan with a coach who knows your situation.
Do you store my company name or sensitive info?+
Anything you share in the chat is stored only for the 30-day coaching window and is used solely to personalise your coaching. We never sell or share your data. You can export or delete it at any time from inside your paid dashboard.
What about a job offer I'm negotiating?+
Different product, different dynamics (counter-offers, equity, sign-on, levelling). We're building it. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know when it's live.
